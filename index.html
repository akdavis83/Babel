The Tower of Babel

The Tower of Babel: They built unto heaven as a pretense for building unto self but they never intended to go beyond the 2nd heaven. They had no interest in heaven or God, they were only interested in making a name for themselves. They scoped out a place, got some super sticky slime and began making bricks for a tower. As our world becomes smaller we see the reversal of the scattering and we see a new end time resurgence of the urge and tendency to gather together for evil against God. Most are not bold enough to openly rebel and so the show of building towards Him is the same but there is no substance to it. Make no mistake about it, Babel was open defiance toward God who had already commanded the people to spread and go from Eden. What are we building and why? To who are we truly building? Are we kingdom building or building our own little kingdoms? What is our heart posture as we build? Unity is our greatest strength that goes mostly untapped and unutilized. True relationship with God is pleasing in His sight, it is real, and it is powerful to walk with God with a pure heart fervently. We are gathered in Christ or scattered abroad.
